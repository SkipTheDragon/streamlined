# Streamlined
✨ Connecting Documentations with Products 

Writing directions and making tutorials for your customers take a lot of time that you might want to invest somewhere else, but neglecting it would mean losing capital and thats not good for business. With Streamlined you provide a link and call it a day!  

[Demo](https://streamlined.wyverr.com)
[Docs](https://wyverr.com/docs/)

  
## Let me give you an example:  
  
**We have the following problem:** 

How you would usually tell your userbase/customers to activate registration on their wordpress website? Well it should look like this:  (P.S.: this was copied from an online article)
  
*1. Login to the Admin section of your WordPress  
2. Roll your mouse over the Settings link in the navigation menu, then click General.  
3. Click the check box next to Anyone can register.
4. Click the Save Changes button. You will then see a message stating Settings saved.*

**Solution:** 

Bleah, too many words, too much wasted time when you can just do this:  

*Press the following button: [![Redirect to your WordPress Instance](https://streamlined.wyverr.com/get-svg/wordpress/My%20General%20Settings)](https://streamlined.wyverr.com/pointing-to/wordpress/sadas) and tick the "*Anyone can register*" box.*
  
**Explanation:**
Right now we did an amazing thing. We created a process that could ease most of if not all of your client's concerns like: "Am i doing this right?", "Did I miss a step?", "I can't find the checkbox" etc.

**Other things you can do with this tool:**
- Automatically complete forms if the website allows it
- Automatically scroll to a part of the page
- Highlight text
